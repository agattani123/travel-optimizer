Jaipur is the capital city of the Indian state of Rajasthan and is also known as the Pink City due to the buildings, being, mostly well, pink!.
Tourist attractions: Hawa Mahal: Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is a beautiful palace located in the heart of Jaipur. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists.
City Palace: City Palace is a beautiful palace complex located in the center of Jaipur. It is a perfect blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architectural styles and is a popular spot for tourists.
Amer Fort: Amer Fort, also known as Amber Fort, is a magnificent fort located on a hilltop near Jaipur. It is known for its beautiful architecture, intricate carvings, and stunning views.
Jantar Mantar: Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory located in Jaipur. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is known for its unique architecture and astronomical instruments.
Jal Mahal: Jal Mahal is a beautiful palace located in the middle of Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists.
Weather: Jaipur has a semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 45 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Jaipur is from July to September, and it brings some relief from the heat. The best time to visit Jaipur is between October and March when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.
