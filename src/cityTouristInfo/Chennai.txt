Chennai, also known as the "Gateway to South India," is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
Tourist Attractions: Marina Beach: Marina Beach is a beautiful and popular beach located in the heart of Chennai. It is the second-longest urban beach in the world and is known for its scenic beauty.
Kapaleeswarar Temple: Kapaleeswarar Temple is a Hindu temple located in the Mylapore neighborhood of Chennai. It is known for its unique architecture and intricate carvings.
Fort St. George: Fort St. George is a historical monument located in the heart of Chennai. It was built by the British East India Company in the 17th century and is known for its unique architecture and historical significance.
Mahabalipuram: Mahabalipuram is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located about 60 kilometers from Chennai. It is known for its ancient temples, rock-cut caves, and beautiful beaches.
Weather: Chennai has a tropical climate with hot summers and cool winters.
The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius.
The monsoon season in Chennai is from October to December, and it can rain heavily during this time.
The best time to visit Chennai is between November and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 20 to 30 degrees Celsius.
