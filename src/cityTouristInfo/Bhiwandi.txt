Bhiwandi is a city, near Thane city in the Thane district in Maharashtra, India.
It is located 20 kilometres northeast of Mumbai and 15 km northeast of the city of Thane.
Tourist Attractions: Tungareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary: A protected area located near Bhiwandi, known for its rich flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and several bird species.
Mahuli Fort: A hill fort located near Bhiwandi, known for its scenic views and trekking opportunities.
Titwala Ganesh Mandir: A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, located in Titwala, a town near Bhiwandi.
Birla Mandir: A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, located in the nearby city of Thane.
Upvan Lake: A popular lake and park located in the nearby city of Thane, known for its serene surroundings and boating facilities.
Sanjay Gandhi National Park: A protected area located near Bhiwandi, known for its rich biodiversity, including several bird and mammal species.
Weather: The city has a tropical climate, with hot and humid summers and mild winters. The best time to visit Bhiwandi is between November and February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
