Prayagraj, formerly Allahabad, is a metropolis in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.
Tourist Attractions: Triveni Sangam: A sacred confluence of three rivers, the Ganges, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati, where millions of pilgrims come to take a holy dip during the Kumbh Mela festival.
Allahabad Fort: A historic fort built in the 16th century, known for its impressive architecture and significant role in Indian history.
Anand Bhawan: A famous museum and former residence of the Nehru-Gandhi family, showcasing the life and legacy of India's first Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru.
Khusro Bagh: A beautiful garden and tomb complex, known for its impressive Mughal-era architecture and stunning floral displays.
All Saints Cathedral: A historic cathedral built in the 19th century, known for its beautiful stained glass windows and intricate stone carvings.
Allahabad Museum: A popular museum showcasing the art, culture, and history of Allahabad and Uttar Pradesh.
Alopi Devi Mandir: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Sati, known for its spiritual significance and stunning architecture.
Weather: The city has a humid subtropical climate, with hot summers and cool winters. The best time to visit Allahabad is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
