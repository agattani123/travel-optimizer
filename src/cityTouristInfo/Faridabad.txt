Faridabad is a city located in the northern Indian state of Haryana, known for its industrial growth and proximity to the national capital, New Delhi.
Tourist attractions: Surajkund: Surajkund is a famous tourist spot located in Faridabad. It is known for its beautiful lake, lush green surroundings, and the annual Surajkund International Crafts Mela, which attracts visitors from all over the world.
Badkhal Lake: Badkhal Lake is a famous lake located in Faridabad. It is known for its scenic beauty, boating facilities, and peaceful surroundings.
Raja Nahar Singh Palace: Raja Nahar Singh Palace is a famous palace located in Faridabad. It is known for its beautiful architecture, intricate carvings, and historical significance.
Shirdi Sai Baba Temple: Shirdi Sai Baba Temple is a famous temple located in Faridabad. It is known for its spiritual significance, beautiful architecture, and peaceful surroundings.
Dhauj Lake: Dhauj Lake is a famous lake located near Faridabad. It is known for its scenic beauty, adventure activities like rock climbing and trekking, and peaceful surroundings.
Weather: Faridabad has a semi-arid climate with hot summers and cool winters. The temperature during the summer months, from April to June, can reach up to 45 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Faridabad is from July to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The best time to visit Faridabad is between October and March, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.
