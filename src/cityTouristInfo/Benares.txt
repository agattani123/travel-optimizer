Benares is a town located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India.
Tourist Attractions: Kashi Vishwanath Temple: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, with beautiful architecture and spiritual significance.
Dashashwamedh Ghat: A popular ghat (riverfront) on the Ganges River, known for its spectacular evening aarti (worship) ceremony.
Sarnath: An important Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon.
Manikarnika Ghat: A ghat on the Ganges River known for its cremation ceremonies and spiritual significance.
Ramnagar Fort: A historic fort located across the river from Benares, known for its beautiful architecture and museum.
Bharat Kala Bhavan Museum: A museum showcasing the art and culture of India, with a vast collection of paintings, sculptures, and textiles.
Benares Hindu University: A prestigious university founded in 1916, known for its beautiful campus and rich history.
Weather: The city has a humid subtropical climate, with hot summers and cool winters. The best time to visit Benares is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
