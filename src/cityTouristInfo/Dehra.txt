Dehra, which is a small town located in the Kangra district of the northern state of Himachal Pradesh in India. Here are some of the top tourist attractions in Dehra:
Kangra Fort: Kangra Fort is a famous historical site located near Dehra. It is a massive fort built on a hilltop and is known for its beautiful architecture and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
Masroor Rock Cut Temple: Masroor Rock Cut Temple is a famous temple located near Dehra. It is known for its beautiful rock-cut architecture and is believed to have been built in the 8th century AD.
Kareri Lake: Kareri Lake is a beautiful lake located near Dehra. It is surrounded by lush green forests and is a popular spot for trekking and camping.
Chamunda Devi Temple: Chamunda Devi Temple is a famous Hindu temple located near Dehra. It is dedicated to the goddess Chamunda and is known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful surroundings.
Bhagsunag Waterfall: Bhagsunag Waterfall is a beautiful waterfall located near Dehra. It is surrounded by lush green forests and is a popular spot for picnics and day trips.
Weather: Dehra has a moderate climate throughout the year. The summer season in Dehra, from March to June, can be hot with temperatures ranging from 25 to 35 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Dehra is from July to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The winter season in Dehra, from December to February, is relatively cool and pleasant with temperatures ranging from 5 to 20 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Dehra is between October and June when the weather is pleasant and cool.
