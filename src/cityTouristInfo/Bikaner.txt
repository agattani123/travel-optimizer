Bikaner is a city located in the western part of the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is known for its rich history and cultural heritage, as well as for its exquisite handicrafts, vibrant fairs, and mouth-watering cuisine. Here are some of the top tourist attractions and information about the weather in Bikaner:
Tourist attractions: Junagarh Fort: A grand 16th-century fort known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings.
Karni Mata Temple: A unique temple dedicated to the goddess Karni Mata, where rats are considered sacred and allowed to roam free.
Lalgarh Palace: A stunning palace built in the Indo-Saracenic style, now converted into a luxury hotel.
Gajner Palace and Wildlife Sanctuary: A former royal hunting lodge now converted into a hotel and surrounded by a wildlife sanctuary.
Bikaner Camel Festival: A popular annual festival that celebrates the beauty and importance of camels in Rajasthan.
