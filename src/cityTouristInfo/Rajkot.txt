Rajkot is a city located in the western Indian state of Gujarat. Here are some of the top tourist attractions and information about the weather:
Tourist Attractions: Watson Museum - A museum showcasing a wide range of artifacts related to the history of the city and the region.
Kaba Gandhi No Delo - The childhood residence of Mahatma Gandhi, now converted into a museum and memorial.
Rotary Dolls Museum - A museum featuring a large collection of dolls and puppets from around the world.
Aji Dam - A popular tourist spot featuring a large reservoir, garden, and a zoo.
Race Course Grounds - A popular park and sports venue with lush greenery, jogging tracks, and picnic areas.
Weather: Rajkot has a hot semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters. The summer season lasts from March to June, with temperatures ranging from 25 to 42 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season lasts from July to September, bringing moderate to heavy rainfall. The winter season lasts from November to February, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 30 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Rajkot is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and dry.
