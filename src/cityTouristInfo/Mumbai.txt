The financial hub of India and the home of Bollywood and the biggest airport “Chattrapati Shivaji International.”
I would be careful of the street food and the heat though! Pack plenty of water while roaming the city.
Average Weather: 32 °C (90 °F) in summer and 30 °C (86 °F) in winter
Tourist Attractions: Marine Drive, Gateway of India, Chowpatty Beach, Siddhivinayak Temple,
Juhu Beach, Dharavi Slum, Worli Seaface, Haji Ali, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus,
Aksa Beach, Versova Beach, Colaba Causeway Market, Chor Bazaar,
Powai Lake, Kanheri Caves, Gorai Beach, Shivaji Park
