Weather: Haora has a tropical climate with hot and humid summers and mild winters. 40 °C otherwise being between 10 to 25°C. Monsoon is in between June to September with heavy rains, so be careful!
Tourist attractions: Belur Math: Belur Math is a temple complex founded by the great renowned scholar Swami Vivekananda.
Vidyasagar Setu: Vidyasagar Setu is a cable-stayed bridge that spans the Hooghly River and connects Haora with Kolkata.Has scenic views of the city and is flocked by tourists.
Santragachi Jheel: Santragachi Jheel is a large lake located in Haora. It is a popular spot for birdwatching and is home to a variety of migratory birds during the winter months.
Botanical Garden: The Botanical Garden in Haora is a large garden that houses a variety of plants and trees from around the world. It is a popular spot for nature lovers and is also home to a large greenhouse.
