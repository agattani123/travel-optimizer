Saharanpur is a city located in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India.
Tourist Attractions: Shakumbhari Devi Temple: Shakumbhari Devi Temple is a famous temple located in Saharanpur. It is known for its religious significance and beautiful architecture.
Saharanpur Botanical Gardens: Saharanpur Botanical Gardens is a popular tourist destination in the city. It is known for its beautiful gardens and rare species of plants and trees.
Kali Temple: Kali Temple is a famous temple located in Saharanpur. It is known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.
Ch. Jagannath Smriti Museum: Ch. Jagannath Smriti Museum is a popular museum located in Saharanpur. It is known for its historical significance and unique exhibits.
Ghanta Ghar: Ghanta Ghar is a famous clock tower located in the heart of Saharanpur. It is known for its beautiful architecture and historical significance.
Weather:Saharanpur has a humid subtropical climate with hot summers and cool winters. The summer season in Saharanpur, from March to June, can be hot and humid with temperatures ranging from 30 to 45 degrees Celsius. The winter season in Saharanpur, from November to February, is relatively cool and pleasant with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Saharanpur is between October and February when the weather is pleasant and cool.
