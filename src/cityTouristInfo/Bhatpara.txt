Bhatpara is a small town located in the North 24 Parganas district of the Indian state of West Bengal, and it doesn't have many tourist attractions. However, you can visit nearby cities like Kolkata and Barrackpore, which have several tourist attractions.
As for the weather, Bhatpara has a tropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit Bhatpara is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is pleasant.
