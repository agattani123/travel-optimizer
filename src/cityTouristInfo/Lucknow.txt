Lucknow is a city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Here are some of the top tourist attractions and information about the weather:
Tourist Attractions:
Bara Imambara - A stunning maze-like complex featuring a mosque, courtyards, and a labyrinthine hall called Bhulbhulaiya.
Chota Imambara - A striking palace complex, featuring domes, turrets, and elaborate Islamic calligraphy.
Rumi Darwaza - A beautiful 60-feet tall gate with intricate designs, built in the 18th century.
Lucknow Zoo - A popular destination for families, featuring a wide variety of animals and birds.
British Residency - A historical site that played a key role in the Indian Rebellion of 1857.
Weather:
Lucknow has a humid subtropical climate with hot summers and cool winters. The monsoon season lasts from June to September, bringing heavy rainfall to the city. The best time to visit Lucknow is from October to March when the weather is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.
