Guntur is a city located in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, known for its historical and cultural significance.
Tourist attractions: Amaravati Buddhist Site: Amaravati Buddhist Site is a famous archaeological site located in Guntur. It is known for its beautiful stupas, ancient relics, and historical significance.
Kondaveedu Fort: Kondaveedu Fort is a famous historical fort located near Guntur. It is known for its beautiful architecture, panoramic views, and historical significance.
Uppalapadu Bird Sanctuary: Uppalapadu Bird Sanctuary is a famous bird sanctuary located in Guntur. It is known for its diverse bird species, beautiful surroundings, and peaceful environment.
Kotappakonda Temple: Kotappakonda Temple is a famous temple located near Guntur. It is known for its spiritual significance, beautiful architecture, and panoramic views.
Ethipothala Falls: Ethipothala Falls is a famous waterfall located near Guntur. It is known for its scenic beauty, mesmerizing views, and peaceful surroundings.
Weather: Guntur has a tropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 45 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Guntur is from July to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The best time to visit Guntur is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 30 degrees Celsius.
