Pimpri is a suburb of Pune city in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is an industrial town with several manufacturing units, but there are also a few tourist attractions in and around Pimpri. Here are some of the popular tourist attractions and information about the weather:
Tourist attractions in Pimpri: Pimpri Chinchwad Science Park: A great place for science enthusiasts, it has interactive exhibits and demonstrations on various scientific concepts.
Bird Valley Udyan: This park is home to several species of birds and is a great place to relax and enjoy nature.
Bhakti Shakti: It is a cultural center that showcases various forms of art, music, and dance.
Appu Ghar: A popular amusement park with several thrilling rides and attractions.
Chaturshringi Temple: It is a famous temple located on a hill and is dedicated to the goddess Chaturshringi.
Weather in Pimpri: Pimpri has a tropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The summer months from March to June can be very hot and humid, with temperatures ranging from 35°C to 40°C. Monsoon season starts in June and lasts until September, bringing moderate to heavy rainfall. The best time to visit Pimpri is from October to February when the weather is pleasant and cool.
