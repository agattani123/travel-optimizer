Calicut, also known as Kozhikode, is a city located in the Indian state of Kerala. It has a rich history and is known for its scenic beauty, serene beaches, and cultural heritage. Here are some of the tourist attractions in Calicut:
Tourist Attractions: Kozhikode Beach: Kozhikode Beach is a popular attraction for tourists, located in the heart of the city. It is an ideal spot for relaxing and enjoying the beauty of the sea. The beach is also home to a lighthouse, which offers a stunning view of the surroundings.
Mananchira Square: Mananchira Square is a picturesque park located in the heart of the city. It is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike and is surrounded by several important buildings, including the Town Hall and the Public Library.
Beypore Beach: Beypore Beach is located about 10 kilometers from the city center and is a popular spot for water sports, such as boating and swimming. It is also known for its historical significance, as it was an important trading center during ancient times.
Thusharagiri Waterfalls: Thusharagiri Waterfalls is located about 50 kilometers from the city center and is a popular spot for trekking and hiking. The waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere.
In terms of weather, Calicut has a tropical climate with high humidity throughout the year. The best time to visit Calicut is between October and March, when the weather is relatively cooler and more pleasant. During this time, the temperature ranges from 20 to 30 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Calicut is from June to September, and it can rain heavily during this time. It is advisable to avoid visiting Calicut during the monsoon season, as it can cause travel disruptions and make outdoor activities difficult.
