Ahmadnagar is is a city situated in the Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra, India, about 120 km northeast of Pune
Tourist Attractions: Ahmednagar Fort - Ahmednagar Fort is a historical fortification located in the heart of Ahmednagar city. The fort was built in the 15th century and has been witness to many significant events in Indian history.
Salabat Khan's Tomb - Salabat Khan's Tomb is a 16th-century mausoleum located in Ahmednagar. The tomb is an excellent example of Mughal architecture and is an important historical site in the city.
Anand Dham - Anand Dham is a beautiful temple complex located in Ahmednagar, dedicated to Lord Krishna. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and serene surroundings, making it a popular place of worship for locals and visitors alike.
Shani Shingnapur - Shani Shingnapur is a small village located near Ahmednagar, known for its unique Shani temple. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shani, the Hindu god of Saturn, and is believed to have mystical powers.
Vishal Ganpati Temple - Vishal Ganpati Temple is a popular temple located in Ahmednagar, dedicated to Lord Ganesh. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful idol of Lord Ganesh.
