Kota is a city located in the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is situated on the banks of the Chambal River and is known for its historical landmarks, palaces, and temples. The city has a rich cultural heritage and is a popular destination for tourists.
Some of the popular tourist attractions in Kota are:
City Palace: It is a beautiful palace complex that was built by the Maharao of Kota. The palace is known for its stunning architecture and houses many museums and galleries.
Jag Mandir: It is a beautiful temple located on an island in the Kishore Sagar Lake. The temple is dedicated to Lord Jagdish and is a popular tourist spot.
Kishore Sagar Lake: It is a man-made lake that was built in the 14th century. The lake is surrounded by beautiful gardens and is a popular spot for boating.
Chambal Gardens: It is a beautiful garden that is located on the banks of the Chambal River. The garden is known for its lush greenery and is a popular spot for picnics and family outings.
Garadia Mahadev Temple: It is a famous Shiva temple that is located on the banks of the Chambal River. The temple is known for its stunning views of the river and the surrounding hills.
