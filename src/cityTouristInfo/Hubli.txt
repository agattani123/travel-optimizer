Hubli is a city located in the Indian state of Karnataka, known for its historical and cultural significance.
Tourist attractions: Unkal Lake: Unkal Lake is a famous tourist spot located in Hubli. It is known for its serene surroundings, beautiful sunset views, and water sports activities.
Nrupatunga Betta: Nrupatunga Betta is a famous hill located in Hubli. It is known for its beautiful sunrise and sunset views, trekking trails, and panoramic views.
Siddharoodha Math: Siddharoodha Math is a famous spiritual destination located in Hubli. It is known for its religious significance, beautiful architecture, and peaceful surroundings.
Chandramouleshwara Temple: Chandramouleshwara Temple is a famous temple located in Hubli. It is known for its ancient architecture, historical significance, and religious importance.
Bhavanishankar Temple: Bhavanishankar Temple is a famous temple located in Hubli. It is known for its unique architecture, religious significance, and beautiful surroundings.
Weather: Hubli has a tropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Hubli is from June to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The best time to visit Hubli is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 30 degrees Celsius.
