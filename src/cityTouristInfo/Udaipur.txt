Udaipur is a city located in Rajasthan, India. It is known as the "City of Lakes" and has a lot of lavish Indian weddings.
Weather: Tropical climate with hot summers and chilly winters. In summer, the temperature goes up to 40 °C and between October to March, it swings down to 25°C. The rainy season is between July and September, which is often marked by heavy rains.
Tourist attractions: City Palace: City Palace is a palace complex located on the banks of Lake Pichola. It was built in the 16th century and is a blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture. The palace complex includes several buildings, courtyards, and gardens, and is a popular spot for tourists to explore the rich history and culture of the region.
Lake Pichola: Lake Pichola is a picturesque lake located in the heart of Udaipur. It is surrounded by several important buildings, including the City Palace and the Jag Mandir Palace. Boat rides on the lake are a popular activity for tourists, as it offers a stunning view of the surroundings.
Jagdish Temple: Jagdish Temple is a temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is located in the city center. It was built in the 17th century and is known for its intricate carvings and architecture.
Saheliyon ki Bari: Saheliyon ki Bari is a beautiful garden located in Udaipur. It was built in the 18th century and is known for its fountains, lotus pool, and marble elephants.
