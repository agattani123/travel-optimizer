Kalyan is the 7th biggest city in Maharashtra and 29th in India.
Kalyan also serves as a major railway station for the trains bound to Mumbai.
Tourist Attractions: Kala Talao Lake: A beautiful lake surrounded by lush green hills and a popular spot for picnics and boating.
Durgadi Fort: A historic fort known for its beautiful architecture and stunning views of the surrounding hills.
Mahalaxmi Temple: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Mahalaxmi, with beautiful carvings and architecture.
Birla Mandir: A temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, with beautiful marble carvings and a serene atmosphere.
Metro Junction Mall: A popular shopping mall with a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options.
Titwala Ganesh Mandir: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, with beautiful carvings and architecture.
Tansa Wildlife Sanctuary: A wildlife sanctuary with a variety of flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and deer.
Weather: The city has a tropical wet and dry climate, with hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit Kalyan is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
