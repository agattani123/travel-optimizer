Gulbarga is a city located in the Indian state of Karnataka.
Tourist attractions: Gulbarga Fort: Gulbarga Fort is a famous tourist attraction located in Gulbarga. It is known for its beautiful architecture, historical significance, and breathtaking views.
Khwaja Bande Nawaz Dargah: Khwaja Bande Nawaz Dargah is a famous shrine located in Gulbarga. It is known for its spiritual significance, beautiful architecture, and peaceful surroundings.
Buddha Vihar: Buddha Vihar is a famous Buddhist temple located in Gulbarga. It is known for its ancient architecture, historical significance, and religious importance.
Sharana Basaveshwara Temple: Sharana Basaveshwara Temple is a famous temple located in Gulbarga. It is known for its unique architecture, religious significance, and beautiful surroundings.
Sannati Hill Station: Sannati Hill Station is a famous tourist destination located near Gulbarga. It is known for its beautiful surroundings, trekking trails, and breathtaking views.
Weather: Gulbarga has a tropical climate with hot summers and moderate winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Gulbarga is from June to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The best time to visit Gulbarga is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 30 degrees Celsius.
