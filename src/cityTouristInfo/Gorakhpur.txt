Gorakhpur is a city located in Uttar Pradesh.
Weather: Subtropical climate with hot summers, and cool winters. 45°C in summers but cooler around 15°C in winters. But, be warned, there is a heavy monsoon between June and September.
Tourist attractions: Gorakhnath Temple: Gorakhnath Temple is a famous temple located in Gorakhpur. It is dedicated to Guru Gorakhnath, a famous saint of the Nath Sampradaya. The temple complex also includes a large pond and several smaller temples.
Kushmi Forest Reserve: Kushmi Forest Reserve is a popular spot for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. It is located about 35 kilometers from Gorakhpur and is home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and deer.
Imambara: Imambara is a historical monument located in Gorakhpur. It was built in the 18th century by the Nawab of Awadh and is a popular spot for tourists to explore the rich history and culture of the region.
Geeta Vatika: Geeta Vatika is a park located in Gorakhpur, which is dedicated to Lord Krishna.
