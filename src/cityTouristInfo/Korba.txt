Korba is a city and an industrial area in Korba District in the Indian state of Chhattisgarh.
Tourist Attractions: Kanki Temple: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, with beautiful carvings and architecture.
Hasdeo Barrage: A popular spot for picnics and boating, with beautiful views of the surrounding hills.
Radhika Water Park: A popular water park with a variety of rides and attractions.
Pali Mines Museum: A museum showcasing the history and culture of the mining industry in Korba.
Tattapani Hot Water Springs: A natural hot spring known for its healing properties and scenic surroundings.
Kudurmalai Temple: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, with beautiful carvings and architecture.
Kori Dam: A beautiful dam located on the Hasdeo River, with stunning views of the surrounding hills and a popular spot for picnics.
Weather: The city has a humid subtropical climate, with hot summers and cool winters. The best time to visit Korba is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
