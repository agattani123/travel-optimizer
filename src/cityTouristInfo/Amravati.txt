Amravati is a city located in the Indian state of Maharashtra.
Tourist Attractions: Ambadevi Temple: Ambadevi Temple is a Hindu temple located in the heart of the city. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists and devotees.
Melghat Tiger Reserve: Melghat Tiger Reserve is a wildlife sanctuary located near Amravati. It is known for its diverse flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and various species of birds.
Chikhaldara: Chikhaldara is a hill station located near Amravati. It is known for its scenic beauty, lush green forests, and waterfalls.
Shri Bhakti Dham Temple: Shri Bhakti Dham Temple is a Hindu temple located in the city. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists and devotees.
Weather: Amravati has a tropical climate with hot summers and cool winters.
The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 45 degrees Celsius.
The monsoon season in Amravati is from July to September, and it can rain heavily during this time.
The best time to visit Amravati is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.
