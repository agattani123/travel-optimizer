Jalandhar is the third most-populous city in the Indian state of Punjab and the largest city in Doaba region.
Tourist Attractions: Rangla Punjab Haveli: A cultural center that showcases the traditional Punjabi way of life through art, music, and dance performances.
Devi Talab Mandir: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to goddess Durga, with a large pond where devotees take a dip.
Wonderland Theme Park: A popular amusement park with a variety of rides and attractions.
Pushpa Gujral Science City: A science museum with interactive exhibits and a planetarium.
Shaheed-E-Azam Museum: A museum dedicated to the life of Indian revolutionary Bhagat Singh.
Niku Park: A scenic park with a variety of plants, flowers, and a waterfall.
Shiv Mandir: A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, with intricate carvings and beautiful architecture.
Weather: The city has a humid subtropical climate, with hot summers and cool winters. The best time to visit Jalandhar is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is cooler and more pleasant.
