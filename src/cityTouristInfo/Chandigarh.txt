Chandigarh is a union territory and planned city in northern India, serving as the shared capital of the states of Punjab and Haryana
Tourist Attractions: Rock Garden: A sculpture garden made out of recycled materials.
Sukhna Lake: A man-made lake that offers boating and other water sports.
Rose Garden: A garden with thousands of different varieties of roses.
Open Hand Monument: A symbolic structure designed by Le Corbusier, the architect who designed Chandigarh.
Capitol Complex: A government administrative building designed by Le Corbusier.
Chattbir Zoo: A large zoo with a wide range of animals and birds.
Pinjore Gardens: A Mughal style garden with fountains, waterfalls, and beautiful landscaping.
Weather: Chandigarh is a city in the northern part of India and has a subtropical climate with hot summers and cold winters. The best time to visit Chandigarh is from October to March when the weather is mild and pleasant.
