Tiruppur or Tirupur is a city in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Tiruppur is the administrative headquarters of Tiruppur district and the Seventh largest city as well as an urban agglomeration in Tamil Nadu.
Tourist Attractions: Noyyal River - The Noyyal River is a beautiful and serene spot in Tiruppur, and is a great place to visit if you want to relax and enjoy the scenic beauty of the area.
Koolipalayam Wetlands - The Koolipalayam Wetlands is a unique ecological site in Tiruppur that is home to a variety of birds and other wildlife. It is a great place to visit if you are interested in nature and bird watching.
Amaravathi Crocodile Farm - The Amaravathi Crocodile Farm is a popular tourist attraction in Tiruppur. It is home to a large number of crocodiles, and visitors can watch them in their natural habitat.
Thirumoorthy Hill Temple - The Thirumoorthy Hill Temple is a popular pilgrimage site in Tiruppur. It is located at the foothills of the Thirumoorthy Hills, which offer stunning views of the surrounding area.
Weather: Tropical climate, with hot and humid weather throughout most of the year. The city experiences three main seasons: summer, monsoon, and winter.
Summer in Tiruppur lasts from March to May, and temperatures can soar as high as 40°C (104°F) during this time. It is a dry season with low humidity and clear skies.
