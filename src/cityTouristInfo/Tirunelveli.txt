Tirunelveli is a city located in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
Tourist attractions: Nellaiappar Temple: Nellaiappar Temple is a Hindu temple located in the heart of Tirunelveli. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists and devotees.
Agasthiyar Falls: Agasthiyar Falls is a beautiful and scenic waterfall located near Tirunelveli. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for trekking and picnics.
Courtallam Falls: Courtallam Falls is another beautiful waterfall located near Tirunelveli. It is known for its natural beauty and is a popular spot for swimming and picnics.
Manimuthar Falls: Manimuthar Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Tirunelveli. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for trekking and picnics.
Weather: Tirunelveli has a tropical climate with hot and humid summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Tirunelveli is from June to September, and it can rain heavily during this time. The best time to visit Tirunelveli is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 20 to 30 degrees Celsius.
