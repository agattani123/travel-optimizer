Tourist Attractions: Udayagiri Fort - Udayagiri Fort is a historical landmark in Nellore, built during the 17th century by the Vijayanagara Empire. The fort is known for its impressive architecture and beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.
Jonnawada - Jonnawada is a small village located near Nellore, known for its ancient temple dedicated to the goddess Sri Padmavathi Ammavari. The temple is a popular pilgrimage site and is believed to have been built during the 12th century.
Pulicat Lake - Pulicat Lake is a beautiful lagoon located near Nellore, known for its scenic beauty and diverse birdlife. Visitors can enjoy boating and birdwatching in the lake, and the nearby Pulicat Bird Sanctuary is a popular attraction for nature enthusiasts.
Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple - Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple is an ancient temple located in Nellore, dedicated to Lord Ranganatha. The temple is known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings, and it is a popular place of worship for locals and visitors alike.
