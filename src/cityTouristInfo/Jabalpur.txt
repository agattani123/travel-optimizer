Jabalpur is a city located in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. Here are some popular tourist attractions and information about the weather in Jabalpur:
Tourist attractions: Bhedaghat - A popular destination for its marble rocks, waterfalls, and the Narmada River.
Dhuandhar Falls - A popular waterfall located in the Bhedaghat area.
Madan Mahal Fort - A historic fort with a panoramic view of the city.
Rani Durgavati Museum - A museum dedicated to the life and history of the queen Rani Durgavati.
Dumna Nature Reserve - A wildlife sanctuary and nature reserve with a variety of animals and birds.
Weather: The weather in Jabalpur is generally hot and humid for most of the year. The summer months (March to June) are particularly hot, with temperatures ranging from 30°C to 45°C (86°F to 113°F). The monsoon season (July to September) brings heavy rainfall and slightly cooler temperatures, ranging from 23°C to 33°C (73°F to 91°F). The winter months (October to February) are cool and dry, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 28°C (50°F to 82°F).
