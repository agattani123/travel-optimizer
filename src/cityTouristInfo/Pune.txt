Tourist Attractions: Shaniwar Wada: Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification located in the heart of Pune. It is known for its beautiful architecture and historical significance.
Aga Khan Palace: Aga Khan Palace is a popular tourist destination in Pune. It is known for its beautiful architecture and historical significance as it was once the prison where Mahatma Gandhi was held.
Osho International Meditation Resort: Osho International Meditation Resort is a popular meditation center located in Pune. It is known for its peaceful atmosphere and offers a wide range of meditation and wellness programs.
Parvati Hill: Parvati Hill is a popular tourist destination in Pune. It offers stunning views of the city and has several temples and historical landmarks.
Sinhagad Fort: Sinhagad Fort is a famous fortification located on a hilltop near Pune. It is known for its historical significance and offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
Weather: Pune has a moderate climate throughout the year. The summer season in Pune, from March to May, can be hot with temperatures ranging from 35 to 40 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Pune is from June to September, and it brings moderate to heavy rainfall to the region. The winter season in Pune, from November to February, is relatively cool and pleasant with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Pune is between October and February when the weather is pleasant and cool.
