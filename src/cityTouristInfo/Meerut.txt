Meerut is a city located in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It has a rich history and culture, and there are several tourist attractions in and around the city.
Some of the popular tourist attractions in Meerut are:
Augarnath Temple
Jama Masjid
Shahpeer Sahab ki Dargah
St. John's Church
Hastinapur Wildlife Sanctuary
Sardhana Church
Mansa Devi Temple
Suraj Kund Park
Bale Miyan ki Dargah
Weather: Meerut has a hot semi-arid climate. The summer season is very hot and lasts from April to June, with temperatures ranging from 35-45°C. The monsoon season begins in July and lasts until September, with occasional heavy rainfall. The winter season is relatively mild and lasts from November to February, with temperatures ranging from 7-25°C. The best time to visit Meerut is from October to March when the weather is pleasant.
