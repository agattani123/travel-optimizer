Patna, the capital city of Bihar, is known for its rich history, culture, and cuisine. Here are some popular tourist attractions and information about the weather:
Tourist Attractions: Mahavir Mandir: A famous temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, visited by millions of devotees every year.
Gandhi Maidan: A popular public park where various cultural and political events are held.
Patna Museum: An excellent collection of artifacts showcasing the rich history of Bihar.
Takht Sri Patna Sahib: A holy shrine for Sikhs, where Guru Gobind Singh was born.
Kumhrar: An archaeological site that dates back to the Mauryan period.
Weather: Patna experiences a subtropical climate, with hot summers and cool winters. The best time to visit Patna is between October and February when the weather is pleasant and suitable for sightseeing. Summers, which last from March to June, are scorching with temperatures soaring up to 40°C. The monsoon season, which starts in July and lasts till September, brings relief from the heat but also sees heavy rainfall. The winter months, from December to February, are chilly with temperatures dropping to as low as 5°C.
