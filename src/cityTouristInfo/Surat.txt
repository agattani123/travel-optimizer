Surat is a city located in the western India, in the state of Gujarat. It is known for its textile and diamond industries, as well as its rich history and culture. There is alot of industry and educational spots in this developed city.
Tourist Attractions: Dumas Beach: Dumas Beach is a popular tourist spot located along the Arabian Sea. It is known for its black sand and beautiful views of the sea.
Surat Castle: Surat Castle is a historical monument located in the heart of the city. It was built in the 16th century and is known for its unique architecture and historical significance.
Jagdishchandra Bose Aquarium: Jagdishchandra Bose Aquarium is the first multidisciplinary underwater aquarium in the country. It has a variety of marine life, including different species of fish, turtles, and sharks.
Chintanamani Jain Temple: Chintanamani Jain Temple is a famous temple located in the city. It is known for its religious significance, beautiful architecture, and peaceful surroundings.
Science Centre: Science Centre is a popular science museum located in Surat. It is known for its interactive exhibits, planetarium, and science shows.
Weather: In terms of weather, Surat has a tropical climate with hot and humid summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Surat is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 25 degrees Celsius.
