Mangalore is a beautiful coastal city located in the southwestern state of Karnataka in India. The city is known for its beautiful beaches, historical temples, and delicious cuisine.
Tourist Attractions: Panambur Beach: Panambur Beach is a popular beach located in the city. It is known for its clean sand, beautiful views of the Arabian Sea, and various water sports activities.
Sri Mangaladevi Temple: Sri Mangaladevi Temple is a famous temple located in the heart of the city. It is known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.
Kadri Manjunatha Temple: Kadri Manjunatha Temple is a historic temple located on a hill in Mangalore. It is known for its unique architecture and beautiful surroundings.
St. Aloysius Chapel: St. Aloysius Chapel is a famous church located in the city. It is known for its beautiful paintings and exquisite architecture.
Pilikula Nisargadhama: Pilikula Nisargadhama is a popular eco-tourism destination located in Mangalore. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, gardens, and wildlife sanctuary.
Weather: Mangalore has a tropical monsoon climate with moderate temperatures throughout the year. The summer season in Mangalore, from March to May, can be hot and humid with temperatures ranging from 25 to 35 degrees Celsius. The winter season in Mangalore, from December to February, is relatively mild and pleasant with temperatures ranging from 20 to 30 degrees Celsius. The best time to visit Mangalore is between October and February when the weather is pleasant, and the humidity levels are low.
