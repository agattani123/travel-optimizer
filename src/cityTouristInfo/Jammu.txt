Jammu is in the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir.
Tourist Attractions: Vaishno Devi Temple - Vaishno Devi Temple is a famous Hindu pilgrimage site located in the Trikuta Mountains near Jammu. The temple is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Vaishno Devi and is visited by millions of pilgrims every year.
Mubarak Mandi Palace - Mubarak Mandi Palace is a historic palace complex located in the heart of Jammu city. The palace was built during the 19th century and is known for its impressive architecture and beautiful gardens.
Raghunath Temple - Raghunath Temple is a Hindu temple located in Jammu city that is dedicated to Lord Rama. The temple is known for its intricate carvings and beautiful architecture.
Bahu Fort - Bahu Fort is a historic fort located on the outskirts of Jammu city. The fort is known for its beautiful gardens and scenic views of the surrounding landscape.
