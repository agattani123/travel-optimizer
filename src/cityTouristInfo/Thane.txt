Thane is a city located in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is a part of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and is known for its beautiful lakes, historical significance, and natural beauty.
Tourist attractions: Upvan Lake: Upvan Lake is a beautiful and popular lake located in the heart of Thane. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for picnics and nature walks.
Kelwa Beach: Kelwa Beach is a beautiful and peaceful beach located near Thane. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing.
Sargam Water Park: Sargam Water Park is a popular water park located in Thane. It is known for its thrilling water rides and is a popular spot for families and children.
Shri Kopineshwar Temple: Shri Kopineshwar Temple is a Hindu temple located in the heart of Thane. It is known for its unique architecture and is a popular spot for tourists and devotees.
Weather: Thane has a tropical climate with hot and humid summers and mild winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 35 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Thane is from June to September, and it can rain heavily during this time. The best time to visit Thane is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 25 degrees Celsius.
