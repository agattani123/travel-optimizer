Moradabad is a major industrial city of Uttar Pradesh and one of India's biggest export hubs.
Tourist Attractions: Jama Masjid - Jama Masjid is a historic mosque located in the heart of Moradabad city. The mosque was built during the Mughal era and is known for its impressive architecture and beautiful interiors.
Moradabad Brass Market - Moradabad is famous for its brass industry, and the city's brass market is a great place to explore local handicrafts and shop for brass souvenirs.
Sai Temple - Sai Temple is a popular Hindu temple located in Moradabad city. The temple is dedicated to Sai Baba and is visited by devotees from all over the country.
Raza Library - Raza Library is a historic library located in Rampur, just outside of Moradabad. The library is known for its impressive collection of rare manuscripts, including several written in Persian and Arabic.
