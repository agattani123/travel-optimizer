Tourist Attractions: The city has a tropical monsoon climate, which means it experiences heavy rainfall and high humidity throughout the year.
The temperature in Cochin remains relatively consistent throughout the year, with average daytime temperatures ranging from 30-35°C (86-95°F) in the summer months (March to June) and 28-32°C (82-90°F) in the winter months (October to February).
Cochin experiences two main seasons: the monsoon season and the dry season. The monsoon season lasts from June to September, and the city receives the majority of its annual rainfall during this time. The rainfall can be heavy and prolonged, which can lead to flooding and waterlogging in some areas.
The dry season in Cochin lasts from December to February and is considered the best time to visit the city. The weather is cool and pleasant, with low humidity and clear skies. This is the peak tourist season in Cochin, as visitors come to enjoy the pleasant weather and explore the city's culture and heritage.
Overall, Cochin's weather is characterized by high humidity, heavy rainfall, and consistent temperatures throughout the year. Visitors are advised to plan their trips according to the weather conditions and pack appropriate clothing for their trip.
