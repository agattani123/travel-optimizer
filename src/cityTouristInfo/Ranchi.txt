Ranchi is a city located in the eastern Indian state of Jharkhand, known for its natural beauty, waterfalls, and national parks.
Tourist attractions: Hundru Falls: Hundru Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Ranchi. It is known for its scenic beauty, with water cascading down from a height of 98 feet.
Rock Garden: Rock Garden is a beautiful garden located in Ranchi. It is known for its unique sculptures and structures made from rocks and stones.
Jagannath Temple: Jagannath Temple is a famous temple located in Ranchi. It is known for its beautiful architecture, intricate carvings, and spiritual significance.
Dassam Falls: Dassam Falls is another famous waterfall located near Ranchi. It is known for its scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings.
Ranchi Lake: Ranchi Lake is a beautiful lake located in the heart of the city. It is known for its scenic beauty, boating facilities, and peaceful surroundings.
Weather: Ranchi has a humid subtropical climate with hot summers and cool winters. The temperature during the summer months, from March to June, can reach up to 40 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season in Ranchi is from July to September, and it brings heavy rainfall to the region. The best time to visit Ranchi is between October and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.
